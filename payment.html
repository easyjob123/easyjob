How to Make a Monero Payment

Get Monero (XMR): Purchase Monero (XMR) from a trusted exchange or marketplace using your preferred payment method.

Payment: You will be provided with a unique payment address for the transaction. Kindly send the precise amount of XMR to the provided payment address and remember to include the Transaction ID when sending us an email.

Confirmation: Upon payment confirmation on the Monero blockchain, we will proceed with processing your order. Our team will then reach out to you to discuss the steps recorder file and ensure a comprehensive understanding of your project.
